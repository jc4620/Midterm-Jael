Learn to be a good communicator

The following are the skills necessary to be a good communicator at a debate:

1. Give a chance for the opponents to speak even though you may not agree with all the points

2. Listen to the speech attentively without interfering whenever you disagree

3. Learn the skills to offer counter-arguments to the facts given
